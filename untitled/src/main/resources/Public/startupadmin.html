Business Description

AI-powered analytics platform for e-commerce businesses to optimize customer engagement and sales. Our proprietary algorithms analyze customer behavior patterns and provide actionable insights to increase conversion rates by an average of 23%.

Business Plan

Our business model is based on a SaaS subscription with tiered pricing. We target mid-sized e-commerce businesses with annual revenues between $1M-$10M. Our go-to-market strategy includes content marketing, strategic partnerships with e-commerce platforms, and targeted digital advertising. We project to reach 500 customers by the end of year 2 with an ARR of $2.5M.
